Minecraft Mod to Improve Vanilla Experience Without Losing It

Technologies Involved

Gather New Resources

Delve into caves in search of a precious new ore: Amber! This vibrant orange stone generates in jungle biomes, the product of ancient tree sap.

I Am-ber-y Pleased to Meet You

Amber can be used like Stone to craft a full set of tools (including Knives). This material does even more damage than Diamond, and has greater enchantability, but has a slower mining speed and is very fragile. Use Amber for specific combat scenarios.

Move Over Stone

Obsidian is the tool for massive projects. With almost identical capabilities to Stone tools, Obsidian tools are nothing special, except for their extraordinary duarbility. Each Obsidian tool lasts as long as ~62 Stone tools, making them ideal for large-scale construction.

Skin and Bones

Armor progression will never be the same, as Bone armor offers a true early-game option for players who are less inclined to mine for Iron. Besides, who wouldn't want to wear the ribcage of their enemy?

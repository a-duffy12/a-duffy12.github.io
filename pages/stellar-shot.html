Stellar Shot

Mobile Space Puzzle Game Rooted in Physics

Technologies Involved

Stellar Shot's Main Menu

From here, the player can choose to play a sector, view game options, or exit the application. Options include the ability to remove in-game ads, turn music and sound on or off, view the credits, or reset their game progress.

Level Select Menu

Stellar Shot includes 14 different levels, called Sectors. Start a Sector by clicking on its planet icon. Incomplete Sectors are marked with red numbers, completed Sectors with green numbers. Sectors only become available once the previous Sector is completed.

Gameplay Features

Clickling "HELP" in the level menu brings up this new screen, detailing all of the different gameplay components.

Level Example

In the first Sector of the game, the player must launch the spaceship from Earth to Mars. Launch force is adjusted with the orange slider. Launch angle depends on the time of launch, as the angle changes with the home planet's rotation. These controls are in all Sectors.

Galactic Conquest

First Person Shooter RPG set in a Sci-Fi Universe

Technologies Involved

Begin a Journey

Familiarize yourself with the controls of the game. To the left are the names of the members of the development team. To the right is the "Next" button, which will commence your odyssey into the unknown.

First Person Combat

Wield an arsenal of sci-fi weaponry. Players carry a simple pistol, eternally replenishing grenades, and two other guns of their choosing. Options include easy-to-handle submachine guns, flexible assault rifles, and devastating snipers.

A Myriad of Enemies

A horde of enemies stand in your way. Flesh enemies fire at you, but are scorched with Plasma (orange). Armor enemies sponge damage, yet get shredded by Railguns (purple). Robots seek you out to detonate in your face, but are rendered useless by EMPs (blue).

Make Your Own Luck

Conquerors do not rely on good fortune to yield success, they make their own. Utilize your bouts of temporary invulnerability to engage enemies ("E"). Use collected health kits to heal off any wounds that you may have acquired ("H").

Build a Legacy

Defeating enemies and completing missions rewards you with XP. Every 50 XP, open the Skill Points Menu ("=") and increase you offensive, defensive, or utilitarian prowess. Each skill can be upgraded

Final Test

Your journey culminates as you face off against the entity that has put you up to all these tasks. However, they have boundless resources and malovlent intent. Will you be able to stand up to them, or will you be just another trampled flower?
